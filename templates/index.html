Our story

Welcome to MBINGLO FC! We are a passionate group of individuals dedicated to nuturing young and talented in their football journey. Our team was created in 2017 by Fonban Rodrick alias Shey Mbinglo.It was created with the sole purpose of providing a platform for young, aspiring footballers to grow, develop their skills, and persue their dreams in the world of Football!

Our Mission

The primary aim of MBINGLO FC was to promote the general wellbeing of the young and talented players to grow and at the same time to enhance their Technical, Tactical, physical and social skills that are typically of football.

Bellow are some of the missions of MBINGLO FC

  • To inspire and encourage young talented players to grow in the world of football
  • To bring people together as one.
  • It brings about team work, discipline and supporting each other which is the key for good society
  • It brings good behavior for better society just like many other sports played in our community
